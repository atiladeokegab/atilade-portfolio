# IBM Z Society, University of Manchester

## Overview

Founded and led the IBM Z Society UoM Chapter from scratch. Built a community of 50+ students around enterprise tech, mainframe infrastructure, and IBM Z.

## Events

Hosted a full programme of industry sessions:

- **Quantum-Safe Resilience** with Zakeer Suleyman (IBM) on post-quantum cryptography threats
- **Autonomous Systems on Z** with Gil Peleg (Geniez AI) on AI at enterprise scale
- **Mainframe Security: The Cyber Whodunnit** with Niall Ashley (Vertali)
- **Introduction to IBM Z** launch event with Ben Davis (IBM)

## Recognition

IBM Z Student Ambassador, Class of 2025-26. One of 50 ambassadors selected worldwide.
